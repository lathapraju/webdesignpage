admission procedure

Admission to the school is mainly in LKG, Admission to other classes depends upon the vacancies that arise due to withdrawals. A child seeking admission in LKG should be 3.5 years old on or before 1st of June of the school Every student should produce a copy of

  1. 1. Birth Certificate
    2. 2.Aadhaar Card
    3.Community Certificate
    4. Transfer Certificate from previous school
    5.Marks Statement of previous class.
